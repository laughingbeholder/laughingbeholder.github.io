The Spelljammer


The name Spelljammer is as old as travel in space itself. In the most ancient surviving texts, the Spelljammer is described as it is today, still carrying the same name then as it does now. Despite it's name, however, it has no clear known relationship to Spelljamming helms which power other ships in Wildspace.

Galleon


A ship made by groundlings for groundlings. The Galleon is typically a craft used during the initial discovery of space travel (slap a helm in just about anything and it'll fly). Dragonfly

The Dragonfly is built by humans and primarily used by humans and elves. One of the workhorses of space, it's speed, size, and maneuverability make it desireable for small parties and important persons. Arguably the best ship to move fast and stay under the radar.
